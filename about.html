About Me

Greetings! My name is Sachidanand Sahu, and I am excited to share my story with you on this about me page. I am a passionate IT Software Developer, specializing in Android and Web Development.and I am always eager to learn new technologies and stay updated with the latest industry trends.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am also proud to have represented India in the BRICS Skill Competition, where I won Bronze Medal in Mobile Application Devlopment. Competing against the best talent from Brazil, Russia, China, and South Africa was a challenging yet rewarding experience that helped me hone my skills and push my limits.

I am also a proud winner of the IndiaSkills Competition, where I secured the Silver Medal in ITSSB. This accomplishment is a testament to my dedication and hard work towards my craft, and I am grateful for the opportunity to showcase my skills on a national platform.

As an NCC Cadet, I have been fortunate to receive training in leadership, teamwork, and discipline, which have helped shape my personality and outlook towards life. The experience has taught me valuable life skills, which I apply in my professional and personal life.

Being a new tech enthusiast, I am always on the lookout for the latest tech advancements and emerging trends. It is exhilarating to imagine the endless possibilities that technology can bring, and I am committed to leveraging technology to make a difference in the world.

In my free time, I enjoy pursuing my hobbies, such as Games. I believe that engaging in hobbies outside of work is crucial for maintaining a healthy work-life balance and mental well-being.

Volunteering is another passion of mine, and I actively participate in NCC Yogadan. To Help people suffering form covid and Lockdowns in 2020-21.

Thank you for taking the time to read about me. I hope this page gives you a glimpse of who I am as a person and what I stand for. If you would like to connect or have any questions, please do not hesitate to reach out.
